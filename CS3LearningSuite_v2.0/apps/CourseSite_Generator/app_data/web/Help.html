Help

Course Site Generator is a desktop Java application for creating and maintaining course websites. The app presents five main pages:Site, Syllabus, Meeting Times, Office Hours, and Schedule. Within the site page, users can edit the details of their page and choose what information will be included in the website. Within the syllabus page, users can edit information pertaining to the syllabus of the course. Within the meeting times page, users can edit the times of lectures, recitations, and labs for a course. Within the Office Hours page, users can add, edit, and remove TAs for the course, as well as display their Office Hours. Within the schedule page, users can create, edit, and maintain the schedule for the duration of the course. The application saves data to a JSON format that can be used by Web pages for displaying site data.

Step 1: Edit Page

Begin by designing the layout of the website, by selecting basic information about the course, and then designing it with icons and CSS files.

Step 2: Edit Syllabus

Use the syllabus tab to edit information pertaining to the structure of the course.

Step 3: Add Meeting Times

Use the tables in the Meeting Times page to add Meeting Times for lectures, recitations, and labs. Information about the room and TAs can also be added.

Step 4: Manage Office Hours

Add TAs in order to organize their Office Hours. Use the Office Hours table to easily organize the TAs and keep track of their individual office hours.

Step 5: Create Schedule

Select the start and end dates for the course. Then proceed to add events to the schedule such as due dates or holidays.
